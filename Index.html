Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including house architecture design, house construction costing, and consultancy for design implementation. Our goal is to provide end-to-end solutions for your housing needs.

What does the house architecture design process involve?

Our house architecture design process begins with a thorough understanding of your requirements, preferences, and budget. We then develop initial concept designs, refine them based on your feedback, and create detailed architectural plans ready for construction.

How do you determine house construction costing?

We calculate house construction costing by considering various factors such as the size of the house, materials used, labor costs, and any additional features or customization. Our team provides transparent and detailed cost estimates to ensure clarity and accuracy.

What sets your consultancy for design implementation apart?

Our consultancy services for design implementation go beyond just providing plans. We offer expert guidance and support throughout the construction process, ensuring that the design vision is translated seamlessly into reality. From selecting contractors to overseeing the construction progress, we are dedicated to achieving the desired outcome.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, we understand the importance of accessibility and convenience, especially in today’s digital age. We offer online consultations and collaboration tools to work with clients remotely, ensuring effective communication and seamless project management regardless of location.

How long does the entire process take from design to construction completion?

The timeline can vary depending on the complexity of the project, regulatory approvals, and other factors. Generally, the process involves multiple stages including initial consultations, design development, approvals, procurement, and construction. We strive to provide realistic timelines and work efficiently to deliver projects on schedule.
